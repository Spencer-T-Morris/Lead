City Assistance!

We are working to end childhood lead poisoning in Grand Rapids. That is why we offer property owners up to $20,000 in aid to remove lead hazards from their homes.

Eligible Homes

  • Located in the City of Grand Rapids
  • Built before 1978
  • Owner-occupied homes or 1-4 unit rental properties
  • Vacant rental units may also qualify

There are different eligibilty requirements for homeowners and landlords. You can see these guidelines below.

Homeowner Eligibilty

  • Own and live in the home
  • Household income is in Annual Income Limits (Click to learn more)
  • A child under age 6 or a pregnant woman lives in the home, or a child under age 6 living in the home receives childcare

Landlord Eligibilty

  • Tenant household income is in Annual Income Limits (Click to learn more)
  • Tenant households have a child under the age of 6 or a pregnant mother
  • All past-due debts to the City (taxes, fines, fees) are paid
  • Pay a co-pay or 10% of the cost to remove the lead (minimum co-pay is $300)
  • Properties must be marketed to families with young children, at affordable rents for at least 3 years after the work is done
  • Landlords or their staff must become EPA-Certified Renovators. If you need this training, call the Rental Property Owners Association at 161-454-3385.
